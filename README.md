## About Me

I'm André Mestre Martins, a Computer Programming student based in Lisbon, Portugal, currently studying at EPAD. I completed my first year with the highest possible grade — 20 out of 20 — in Web Programming, and since then I've been expanding into game development with C# and full stack engineering. I care about writing clean, maintainable code and designing interfaces that feel intuitive rather than just functional, and I enjoy turning ideas into real, working software rather than leaving them as concepts. Outside of coursework, I explore modern web technologies, UI/UX design, and AI-assisted workflows, always looking for better ways to build and to learn. My long-term goal is to grow into a well-rounded Full Stack Developer, master C#, and eventually contribute to open source projects that other people actually use.
